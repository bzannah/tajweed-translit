1. **"How to Read Quran with Tajweed for Beginners"** — targets: "how to read quran with tajweed", "tajweed for beginners", "learn tajweed online free"
2. **"Complete Guide to Tajweed Colours and Their Meanings"** — targets: "tajweed colours", "tajweed colour coding", "tajweed rules colours"
3. **"Surah Al-Fatiha Transliteration with Tajweed"** — targets: "surah fatiha transliteration", "al fatiha english transliteration"
4. **"How to Pronounce Arabic Letters for Quran Reading"** — targets: "arabic pronunciation guide", "quran pronunciation"
5. **"Quran Transliteration vs Translation — What's the Difference?"** — targets: "quran transliteration meaning", "difference between transliteration and translation quran"
6. **Individual surah guides** for the most-searched surahs: Ya-Sin, Al-Mulk, Ar-Rahman, Al-Kahf, Al-Baqarah

### Internal Linking Strategy
- Every blog post links to 3-5 relevant surah pages

- Learn pages link to blog posts and vice versa
- Homepage links to cornerstone content (done)
